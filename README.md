# StrongHuman

Ever felt like your Human was made of soggy noodles instead of raw lifting power?

StrongHuman lets you scale your strength to whatever your heart desires!

Pick things up like a physics god… or just slightly less pathetic. Your choice.

This mod is fully compatible with Multiplayer, as long as the Host has the mod installed!

## Commands

All interaction is done via the in-game console:

### Set strength
`playerstrength <value>`

**Examples:**

`playerstrength 5`  
Sets your strength to 5x (objects feel 5 times lighter).

`playerstrength 0,5`  
Sets your strength to 0.5x (objects feel 2 times heavier).

### Reset strength
`playerstrength reset`

Resets strength back to default (1x).

### Check current strength
`playerstrength`

Displays your current strength value.

## Notes

Setting very high or very low values may cause:

- Physics instability  
- Objects with joints (e.g., cables or ragdolls) may behave unpredictably  
- Strength of 0 is automatically corrected to 1 to avoid breaking physics
